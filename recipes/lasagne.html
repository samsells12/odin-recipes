Pizza

A delicous piece of Lasagne!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional:Basil

Steps

  1. Mix flour,water,salt, and a little olive to make dough
  2. Let dough rest for at least 30min
  3. Lay dough in layers with Tomato sauce in between each layer
  4. Bake for 30 min
