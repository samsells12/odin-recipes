Pizza

An delicous,crispy pizza!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional:Basil

Steps

  1. Mix flour,water,salt, and a little olive to make dough
  2. Let dough rest for at least 30min
  3. Prep dough into circle and lay sauce, cheese, and basil
